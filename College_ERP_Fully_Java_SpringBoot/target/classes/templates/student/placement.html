💡 Placement Preparation Tips
📝
Build a Strong Resume
Keep your resume concise, highlight projects, internships, and skills. Use action verbs and quantify achievements.
💻
Practice Coding Daily
Solve problems on LeetCode, HackerRank or CodeChef. Focus on DSA — arrays, trees, graphs, and dynamic programming.
🤝
Mock Interviews
Practice with peers. Work on communication, confidence, and STAR method answers for HR rounds.
🌐
Build Your LinkedIn
Maintain an updated LinkedIn profile. Connect with alumni and professionals in your target domain.
🔬
Work on Projects
Build real-world projects and push them to GitHub. Recruiters love candidates who have built and shipped things.
📚
Know Your Domain
Study OS, DBMS, CN, and OOP concepts thoroughly. These are asked in almost every technical interview.
